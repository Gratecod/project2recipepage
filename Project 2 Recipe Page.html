Baked Ziti

  • Time:25 minutes
  • Serves:5
Ingredients Details
Pasta,mozarella cheese, milk Add other ingredients
Onions,tomatoes, escallion Oven bake at 350 deg. F

Step 1:Mix and place all ingredients in a large Pyrex dish and cover with foil wrap.

Step 2:Preheat oven to 250 deg. F.Thereafter, place Prex dish with ingredients to bake for 25 minutes.

Step 3:To prevent Ziti from being burned, immediately remove it from the oven when the timer's up and that's it....your tasty Ziti dish is now ready to be savored and eaten!

Pizza Galore

  • Serves:6
Ingredients Details
olive oil,mozarella cheese, milk Add other ingredients
Onions,pasta sauce,dough Oven bake at 350 deg. F

Step 1:Prepare dough by shaping it to the size that suits a family of 6.

Step 2:Toss dough to get desired size for 5 people. Add all ingredients, place in oven and pre-heat to 250 deg. F.

Orange Salmon

  • Time:20 minutes
  • Serves:5
Ingredients Details
Salt, pepper, 1 small orange Add other ingredients
Onions, tomatoes, soysauce Oven bake at 300 deg. F

Step 1:Add all ingredients, marinate with juice from orange and soy sauce and few slices of orange peels. Place ingredients in a deep Pyrex dish and cover with foil wrap.

Step 2:Preheat oven to 250 deg. F, then place Pyrex dish with ingredients to bake for 25 minutes.

Step 3:To prevent Salmon from being overdone, immediately remove it from the oven when the timer's up and walla!....your tasty Orange Salmon dish is now ready to be savored and eaten by everyone!
